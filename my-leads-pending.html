my leads - pending

This Div block will contain a table which contains the list of clients who we have sent the intake to but they have not replied. In three days, if the intake is not filled out, the client will move to the ACTION REQUIRED area of leads.


The data table will go like so:

Date of initial contact | Name of Lead | Category | Status | Deadline | Last Contacted by | Last Contacted on | Notes /// edit leads button /// send lead to action required button

The first three will be immediately filled in from the create client form. (Category corresponds to area of law on the form). Status will automatically be "pending intake". There will be dropdown options for status (pending intake, follow up required {if this is selected immediately move lead to action required} lost {move to historic} bounced {move to historic} retained {move to historic AND to my clients}).

the name of the lead will be a hyperlink to the "leads area" for that lead. See this page

Last contacted by and last contacted on we'll leave blank for now as I haven't decided how/when it will be filled (I might add this to the create client form). Notes will be an editable area in the table where you can write anything
